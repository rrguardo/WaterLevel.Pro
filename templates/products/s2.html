WiFi Water Level S2 - Features

Manual

Features List

  • Measures the water level in tanks up to 4 meters deep.
    Obtain measurements of water tanks or reservoirs on your cell phone online without having to be near the device.
  • Wi-Fi Wireless Water Level Sensor.
    Wherever Wi-Fi Internet coverage reaches or where its use can be extended, the water level sensor can be used.

    No more bluetooth short range problems. This device use your same home WiFi internet, allowing check water level even from the other side of the world while you have internet.

  • Water Level Alerts By Email Or Web UI.

    Get notification alerts if water level in under or over specific % level or if device is offline.

  • USBC Input Power
    Flexible input power, from standard USBC.
    Input power options:
    • Water Level .Pro USBC Solar Energy Module.
    • Standard USBC phone charger.
    • USBC power banks solar powered
    • USBC power banks
  • Responsive Web App Water Level Monitoring
    Monitor your water level from internet everywhere from your cellphone, tablet, laptop or PC.

    View Live Demo Web App

  • Wide measurement range from 2 cm to 400 cm (4 meters). Small 2 cm blind area.

  • Remote Web administration of device parameters.
    Device adjust options:
    • Empty Level: how many centimeters of deep have the water tank, from sensor to the water tank bottom.
    • Top Margin: centimeters of margin from sensor location to maximum water tank level.
      This is useful in many cases, where the water tank is not filled until it is overflowing and has a shut-off valve at a certain margin of the top cover.
    • Water Level Measurement Frequency
      Adjust the level measurement frequency, useful save battery or check faster the flow level.
  • Automatic device firmware updates to incorporate new features and improvements.
  • Designed and tested for outdoor and indoor use.
    Tested and durable outdoors in sun, rain and wind, under normal conditions.
  • 3dBi Gain External 4GHz WiFi Antenna

    Ranges of more than 100 meters outdoors with line of sight. Indoors, a shorter range (around 50 meters) is recommended.
    Compatible with 4GHz WiFi range extenders.

  • Low energy consumption.

    During water level sending by WiFi approximately 75 mA. During waiting/sleep period 0.01 mA

  • Modular design.
    Independent sensor control unit, sensor, energy module and smart switch.
