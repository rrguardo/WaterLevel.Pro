Solar Power Module P1 - Features
P1 Manual
Solar-power with battery backup.

Provides solar energy with backup batteries to keep the water level sensors working without the need for power cables. In places where sunlight hits directly for at least 3 or more hours a day, you can use the solar module specially designed and tested for the WiFi Water Level S1.

Features List

  • Full wireless.

    Not manual recharge necessary during long periods, if device area receive enough sunlight.

  • Strong Design.

    Designed and tested for exteriors, resist rain water, sunlight, and normal climate conditions at exteriors.

  • AA NiMH rechargeable batteries.

    Internal battery use 3x AA NiMH rechargeable batteries. Easy maintenance and backup energy while sunlight decay.

    Ensure use good brand and quality AA NiMH batteries, 2000 mA or more.

    Only use AA NiMH rechargeable batteries.

    If you are not sure which brand of rechargeable AA NiMH batteries to use. Use the Energizer brand, the product was tested with this brand with continuous use for several months without problems.

  • Integrated solar panel, which recharges the batteries during sunny hours

  • USB-C power output cable compatible with S1

    The power output cable use standard USB-C connector and generate a voltage that range from 3.4 V to 4.2 V.

  • USB-C power output connector compatible with the Water Level S1 power input socket moisture shield.
